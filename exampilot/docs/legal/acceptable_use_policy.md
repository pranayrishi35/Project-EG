# Acceptable Usage Policy (AUP)

**Effective Date:** July 15, 2026

This Acceptable Usage Policy ("AUP") outlines the strict rules and behavioral standards required when using the **ExamPilot** platform. This AUP is incorporated by reference into our Terms of Service. By using ExamPilot, you agree to comply with this policy at all times.

---

## 1. Prohibited Activities

To ensure a fair, secure, and performant environment for all users, the following activities are strictly prohibited:

### A. Data Scraping & Automated Exploitation
> [!CAUTION]
> The use of automated bots, spiders, scrapers, or any programmatic scripts to extract, copy, or mine our proprietary question bank, mock tests, or study materials is strictly forbidden. 

### B. AI Manipulation & Prompt Injection
Our platform integrates advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide the "AI Tactical Coach" and study plan generation. You may not:
- Execute or attempt to execute **Prompt Injection attacks** (e.g., attempting to bypass system instructions, extract system prompts, or force the AI to behave outside its intended parameters).
- Exploit or utilize the AI tutor or Gemini API integration for any non-educational purposes (e.g., generating unauthorized content, spam, or bypassing content filters).

### C. Security Breaches & Hacking
Any attempt to compromise the integrity of our platform infrastructure will result in immediate termination and potential legal action. You may not:
- Attempt to bypass, exploit, or breach our **Supabase Row Level Security (RLS)** architecture.
- Access, view, or modify another user's data by any means, including manipulating identifiers, tokens, session data, or API requests (IDOR attacks).
- Reverse-engineer or decompile client-side application code to extract non-public API contracts, scoring logic, or answer keys.
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the ExamPilot system or Vercel hosting environment.
- Interfere with or disrupt the access of any user, host, or network (e.g., DDoS attacks).

### D. Account Abuse
- **Credential Sharing:** You may not share, sell, or rent your account credentials. Your account is for your individual use only.
- **Sybil Abuse:** You are explicitly prohibited from engaging in automated or bulk account creation, or abusing free-tier or credit systems through the use of multiple accounts.

ExamPilot employs automated and manual monitoring to ensure compliance with this AUP. 

> [!IMPORTANT]

Where applicable, ExamPilot also reserves the right to report malicious activities (such as severe data theft or security breaches) to relevant law enforcement authorities.
